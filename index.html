The Scope of Freelance Web Development in Kerala

  • sep, 2024
  • 10 min

Freelancing space is on growth in Kerala due to the good digital ecosystem of the state, a tech-oriented populace, and also growth realizing businesses to digitalize. The situations are more favorable amongst freelance web developers who hail from this place as the region is comparatively well educated, with a sound literacy level and access to the internet, hence gaining a status of the IT hub in India.

Much local SME and startup need lies in creating an online presence, but these skilled freelance developers who can offer customized solutions at a reasonable price are in high demand. Local freelance web developers in Kerala can deliver services like responsive website building and e-commerce sites to mobile applications pertaining to a wide range of industries, tourism, healthcare, education, or retail.

Besides all these, co-working spaces, networking events, and government initiatives such as the Kerala Startup Mission or KSUM have helped expand the freelancing community oriented towards ideas and working together. Such factors bring to freelance web developers a good opportunity not only for enhancing services on an individual basis but for extending their market for cooperation as well as developing new services.

The Scope of Frontend, Backend, and Full Stack Development in Web Development

  • June, 2024
  • 10 min

Now with the advent of the new web development era, frontend development, backend, full stack development holds its specific features and importance in making modern-day websites and applications. Familiarity with each role is very important for wannabe developers and businesses alike who wish to give the best solutions that make great use of technology for customers.

Front-end development focuses on the design-oriented features that a user interacts with. The key duties of the frontend developers include: active usage of HTML CSS & Java Scripts, along with libraries and frameworks like React, Angular, and Vue.js, for developing efficient user interfaces. Involved with the user experience process and designing, front-end developers are responsible for making the website user friendly. With higher scale web and mobile apps getting more adaptations, frontend developers still have bright prospects in e-commerce, media, and more.

However, it is the backend development that gives the real purpose and feature of web apps which includes but is not limited to databases, server-side logic, and application programming interfaces, APIs. Python, C#, Java, Node.js, PHP, and also databases such as MySQL, MongoDB, PostgreSQL among others are the languages and database systems predominantly used by backend developers. Specialisation in their area ensures there is smooth transfer of data within an application and the application works the way one intended it to.
